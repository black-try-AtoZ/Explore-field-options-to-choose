Computer Science

Computer Science is the study of computational systems, programming, and the theoretical underpinnings of how we solve complex problems with machines. Whether you're intrigued by algorithms or fascinated by artificial intelligence, this field lays the foundation for nearly every modern innovation.

What You’ll Learn

  • Programming languages (Python, Java, C++)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems and Computer Architecture
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Software Development & System Design

Career Opportunities

Studying Computer Science opens doors to a range of high-impact roles:

  • Software Developer
  • AI/ML Engineer
  • Database Administrator
  • Quality Assurance Engineer
  • Systems Architect
