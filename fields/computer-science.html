Computer Science

Computer Science is all about how computers work and how we tell them what to do. It's the study of computers, programming, and how to solve problems using technology. You can learn to create apps, websites, and even smart machines. It's like learning the secret language of computers— and once you know it, you can make them do amazing things!

What You’ll Learn

  • Programming languages (C++, Java, HTML, CSS, Javascript, C#)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems and Computer Architecture
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Software Development & System Design
