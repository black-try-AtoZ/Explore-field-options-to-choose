Software Engineering

Software Engineering is the disciplined approach to designing, building, testing, and maintaining software systems. It’s the heartbeat of modern technology—powering everything from mobile apps to enterprise platforms. If you love solving problems and creating tools people use every day, software engineering might be your path.

What You’ll Learn

  • Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)
  • Object-Oriented Programming & Design Patterns
  • Version Control and Collaboration (Git, GitHub)
  • Agile and DevOps Practices
  • Testing, Debugging, and Maintenance
