Information Technology (IT)

Information Technology focuses on using computers, networks, and systems to store, retrieve, transmit, and protect data. It’s the engine that powers businesses, secures digital infrastructure, and connects people globally. IT professionals build, support, and manage the technology that runs the world—one server, cable, and cloud at a time.
